General

Welcome to A.A.N. Japan Co., Ltd. (“AAN Japan”). These Terms and Conditions (“Terms”) apply to all exports of used motor vehicles (“Vehicles”) sold by AAN Japan to customers located outside Japan (“you”). By transferring a deposit for the purchase of Vehicles, you agree to be bound by these Terms.

Our Role

AAN Japan specializes in sourcing, preparing, and exporting used motor vehicles from Japan. We act either as a direct seller from our stock or as an agent facilitating purchase and export of auction vehicles. All Vehicles are sold as is, unless otherwise stated in writing.

Deposit Requirement

To begin doing business with AAN Japan, you must transfer a deposit:

  • Bidding up to JPY 1,000,000: deposit JPY 100,000
  • Bidding over JPY 1,000,000 and up to JPY 5,000,000: deposit JPY 300,000
  • Bidding over JPY 10,000,000: deposit 50% of your requested bidding limit

AAN Japan may require a higher deposit for expensive lots, special equipment, Vehicles of special purpose, or other non-standard lots. The deposit activates your account and enables sourcing/bidding.

The deposit remains on hold with AAN Japan for the duration of our relationship and is not applied to each purchase. It will be credited toward the final invoice of your last Vehicle when you stop doing business with us.

The deposit is refundable on request; you are responsible for all bank fees. Refunds are sent only to the original remitting bank account in your name and processed within 30 business days after a written request. If you refuse or cancel after we secured a Vehicle on your behalf, the deposit is not refundable.

Vehicle Sourcing, Condition & Information

Vehicles are sourced from Japanese auto auctions and/or AAN Japan stock. We may act as a direct seller or as an agent. Availability, pricing, and condition depend on actual auction/seller outcome.

  • All Vehicles are sold strictly as is, unless explicitly stated otherwise in writing.
  • Auction grades, inspection sheets, mileage, and condition reports are for reference only; we do not guarantee accuracy of third-party reports, odometer readings, repair or accident history.
  • Vehicles may contain wear, damage, or modifications not listed in documentation. We are not liable for discrepancies discovered after purchase, loading, or arrival.
  • Some auctions offer paid Pre-Auction Inspection Service (typically up to 3 hours before bidding). We can arrange it upon your request; all costs are yours.
  • Auction sheets and inspection reports are prepared by auctions or their subcontractors; we do not guarantee their accuracy or completeness.
  • Photos, translations, and descriptions provided by AAN Japan are guidance only and not a warranty. You must review all available information before confirming a purchase and accept the recorded condition at the time of purchase.
  • You are solely responsible for compliance with destination import requirements (year, emissions, other restrictions).

Auction Sheet Translations

  • Manual Translation: by AAN Japan staff upon your request.
  • Automatic AI Translation: provided by AAN Japan staff for lots you view on authorized platforms.

Manual translations must be requested in advance; if you purchase without one, you bear full responsibility for any automatic AI translation. If we cannot reach you by all available means, the bid will not be processed. We are responsible for accuracy of our manual translations. AI translations are for reference only. You must review all diagram marks/symbols; AAN Japan has no obligation to highlight them.

Prices & Payments

All prices are in Japanese Yen (JPY) unless stated otherwise. All payments are accounted in JPY. If you pay in USD/other currency, the amount is converted using the MUFJ Bank TTB rate on the funds-received date; exchange differences and bank charges are yours. The applied rate is final.

  • Payments by international bank transfer or another expressly approved method. You cover all bank fees.
  • Full payment within 3 business days of purchase. Late payment may result in cancellation/resale without notice.
  • Installments may be offered; shipment is released only after final payment is received.
  • We may stop shipping, cancel, resell, or re-auction for late/incomplete payments; all related costs/losses are yours.
  • All confirmed payments are final and non-refundable, except where required by law or explicitly stated otherwise.

Additional Transportation Charges

Extra in-Japan charges are your responsibility (non-exhaustive): remote car parks; lowered/raised suspension; non-runners; height > 2m; high roof racks; heavy oil leakage; negotiations/one-price/AUCNET; trucks/special Vehicles; storage > 1 month.

Delivery & Incoterms

  • Incoterms® 2020 (FOB, CFR, CIF) unless otherwise agreed.
  • You select delivery in your account; we confirm it. Risk passes per the chosen rule. If you fail to provide instructions timely, we may arrange shipment at our discretion; all costs/risks are yours.
  • We are not responsible for delays/charges caused by third parties or force majeure. You are responsible for import clearance and compliance.
  • If the selected method is unavailable, choose an alternative or cancel; we are not liable for related losses/costs.

Ro-Ro Shipment

Minimum Condition (Must Have)

  • Ground clearance ≥ 15 cm; hood opens normally.
  • Drivable: engine starts; transmission engages; moves under own power.
  • Steering/tires functional; brakes (service & handbrake) functional.
  • No tow/lift/winch required.
  • Glazing: windshield present/intact; side/rear windows present and securable.
  • No inner cargo (interior/trunk/roof rack empty).

Not Accepted (Cannot Ship Ro-Ro)

  • Non-runner; engine will not start.
  • White/black smoke; fluid leaks.
  • Defective/damaged/flagged HV/EV traction batteries.
  • Missing windshield; missing/non-securable side/rear windows.
  • Any inner cargo or loose items.

Refusals & Charges

Carrier/terminal may refuse any unsafe/non-conforming unit. If refused, you authorize us to rebook after rectification, switch to container (if feasible), or return to yard — all costs are yours.

Container Shipment

  • Requires sufficient units per container; all vehicles must arrive to the same designated yard.
  • Non-runners/heavily damaged units may need special handling (forklift, skates, winch, extra labor) at your cost.
  • Some destinations restrict containers for certain types; you must confirm rules.

Lashing & Securing

Stuffing and securing to industry standards (blocking/bracing, straps, timber chocks, wheel nets) per our stowage plan. After sealing, risk passes per Incoterms; no liability for in-transit movement if plan was followed.

Third-Party Lashing / Stuffing

If you appoint a third party (or instruct us to use one), we act as agent only; no warranty on their work; claims to that provider; all fees are yours.

Overstuffing at Your Instruction

We provide a recommended plan. If you instruct to exceed it, mix incompatible sizes/heights, or require non-standard stacks, you accept risks of contact damage, deformation, paint rub, glass damage, leaks, and tie-down marks. Extra materials/labor/delays/re-stuffing/rebooking are for your account. We may refuse unsafe/non-compliant stuffing.

Video-Open Requirement at Destination

You must unseal/open the container under continuous video (container number, intact seal & seal number, door opening, interior before unstuffing, unstuffing process) and send evidence within 24 hours of first opening/gate-out. Discrepancies must be noted on EIR/delivery receipt and reported to carrier before gate-out; otherwise claims are void.

Additional Shipping Costs

Re-stuffing/re-lashing; extra materials; port storage/demurrage; container detention/per diem; missed-cut or rebooking; terminal handling; crane/forklift/winch; cleaning/decontamination; removal of inner cargo; waste disposal; docs amendments; courier; compliance/re-inspection fees — all for your account.

Pre-Export Inspection (PEI) & Compliance

Mandatory where required by destination. We arrange PEI only on your request and expense. If a vehicle fails, all rectification/re-inspection/storage/rebooking costs are yours.

Export & Shipping Documents

We provide standard export documents after cut-off: Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Export Certificate and any agreed certificates.

For USA: ISF data prepared; you/your broker must submit to CBP; ISF data available for download in your account.

Additional documents (Certificates of Origin, Translation Certificates, etc.) — subject to availability and charged separately.

Original BLs are couriered only after full payment; Surrender BL / Sea Waybill — released electronically after full payment. We are not liable for third-party processing delays.

Lien; Right to Withhold

AAN Japan may retain Vehicles, parts, documents, and/or titles until all amounts due are paid in full.

Risk & Title

Risk passes per Incoterms. Title transfers only after full payment is received by AAN Japan.

Cancellations

Permitted only within windows allowed by the relevant Auction House (generally 30 minutes–2 hours after purchase). If cancellation isn’t possible, the Vehicle will be re-auctioned. You bear all costs, fees, storage, penalties, losses. In rare cases a seller may refuse to complete the sale; we refund your payment for that Vehicle but are not liable for further losses.

Claims

We may submit a claim to the auction if permitted by their rules; acceptance is determined solely by the auction. No guarantee of acceptance or compensation. Auctions do not accept claims for specified categories (e.g., Accident/Low Price/White corners; >150,000 km; < JPY 500,000; heavily modified; > 20 years; other exclusions). After yard arrival, photos are uploaded to your account; review immediately.

Warranties & Exclusions

Vehicles are sold as is, with no warranties unless explicitly stated in writing. No warranty on wear & tear, hidden damage, misuse, odometer accuracy, repair/accident history, or suitability for import/use in your country.

Limitation of Liability

Our maximum liability never exceeds the Vehicle purchase price. We are not liable for delays due to carriers/ports/customs/inspections/strikes/force majeure; indirect or consequential losses (profit, downtime, resale value); errors/omissions in third-party reports, auction sheets, AI translations, or photos. All confirmed sales are final and non-refundable, except as required by law.

Communication & Disputes

Submit complaints first via the Claims section in your account before posting negative/neutral feedback. If feedback is posted without a prior claim, we may treat the transaction as closed and withdraw further assistance.

Compliance & Responsibilities

You must comply with export/import, sanctions, customs, and safety laws. These Terms are subject to AAN Japan Terms of Use, Compliance Policy, Privacy Policy, and Sanctions & Export Control Policy.

Jurisdiction and Applicable Law
